Who Am I?

 

I learned HTML4.01 and CSS2 in junior high as a hobby. Don't ask me how it piqued my interest, though. That's a mystery.

I would take an image, add a <div> color square on top at some opacity for text to be readable, and embed an iframe so the images are loaded only once. Atrocious but it worked.

Transitioning to HTML5 and CSS3 was difficult at first. I initially thought semantic HTML was helpful, but not necessary. Oh, how wrong was I! Flexbox and the other new standards are revolutionary. No more table layouts!

I wonder what the future holds.
